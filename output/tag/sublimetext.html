Just an aide-memoire for myself really. When referring to keybindings then I will use C to indicate Command (OS X) or Control (Windows).

  • C-D expands the current selection to the next word, but it's easy to overshoot. In which case, C-U will undo (called a soft undo, and therefore distinct to C-Z and C-shift-Z). A soft redo is C-shift-U. (tip via Brett Terpstra).
